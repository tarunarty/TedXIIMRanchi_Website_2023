Vicky Ratnani

Gourmand, Connoisseur, Celebrity Chef

Chef Vicky Ratnani, born and raised in Mumbai, has a wit and charm evocative of natural Indian spice. He is a gourmand at heart. Even as a child, his love for food took him into corners of his home where children rarely venture, such as the kitchen! On his culinary tour, Vicky trained approximately 180 cooks at the White Star Chef's Training School.
